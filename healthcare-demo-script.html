"There's no IT project. The dashboard is a web application — your team opens a URL. The API connects to your existing claims export. We can be live with your real data in 2 weeks, not 2 quarters."

"The platform processes claim-level financial data — the same category your clearinghouse and billing vendor handle. We follow the same BAA framework. Happy to walk through the security documentation after the meeting."

"The narrative is generated from your actual claim data — the dollar amounts, denial codes, and timelines are real. The AI structures the analysis and prioritizes actions. Your billing team reviews and executes. It's a copilot, not an autopilot."
