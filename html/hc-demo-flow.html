"Your team is excellent at the denials they know about. The gap is speed — ENC-8821 at 52 days AR is approaching the appeal window. This surfaces it day one. The appeal window is still open. That's the entire value."
"What does implementation look like?"
"No IT project. This is a web application — your team opens a URL. The API connects to your existing claims export. Live with your real data in 2 weeks, not 2 quarters."
"Is the AI narrative accurate?"
"The narrative is generated from your actual claim data — every dollar amount and denial code is real. The AI structures and prioritizes. Your team reviews and executes. It's a copilot, not an autopilot. 94% confidence means it's right 94 times out of 100 — your team catches the other 6."
"What about HIPAA?"
"We process claim-level financial data — the same category your clearinghouse and billing vendor handle today. Same BAA framework. Happy to walk through the security documentation after the meeting."
"What does this cost?"
"Let's talk about what $1.77M in recovered revenue looks like against the investment. But first — who in your organization owns the denial management workflow? That's the conversation I want to have next."
