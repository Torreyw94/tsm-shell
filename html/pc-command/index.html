Documentation Gaps
#1 cause of AZ agent E&O claims. Always document client conversations, coverage recommendations declined, and changes requested in writing.

HOW TO USE P&C ENTERPRISE COMMAND
Complete guide for every tab and tool in this platform
1
Dashboard — Start Here
The Dashboard gives you a live overview of your entire P&C book: coverage gaps, priority alerts, and a full AI briefing. Use the preset buttons (Umbrella Gap Scan, Wildfire Scan, Auto Gap Scan) for instant book-wide analysis. The Coverage Status table shows which lines need attention.
💡 Run the full briefing first thing each morning to catch time-sensitive issues
2
Homeowners Tab
Enter dwelling value, personal property, current coverage, ZIP, and risk factors. Click "Calculate Gap" to see the coverage shortfall instantly. Then run "Analyze Coverage" for AI recommendations including AZ-specific wildfire, monsoon, and pool liability considerations.
💡 The AZ Risk Profile cards show which risks are most common for each ZIP area
3
Auto Tab
Enter vehicle value, current BI limits, driver ages, and violations. The AI identifies minimum-limit gaps (the #1 P&C exposure in AZ), recommends proper limits, and flags teen driver and rideshare exposures. The liability gap table shows real-dollar exposure at minimum limits.
💡 AZ minimum $25K/$50K leaves clients personally liable for hundreds of thousands — always recommend $100K/$300K minimum
4
Commercial Tab
Select business type, revenue, and employee count. Get CGL limit recommendations, workers comp requirements, and a cyber liability gap analysis. The cyber assessment is separate — run it for any client handling PII, PHI, or payment data. AZ cannabis businesses have unique P&C needs covered here.
5
Umbrella Tab
Enter net worth, income, and risk factors (pool, teen drivers, rentals). The calculator recommends minimum umbrella limits and shows the ROI table — most clients are shocked how cheap umbrella coverage is relative to the protection it provides. Use the "Underlying Requirements" output to ensure auto and HO limits are umbrella-qualifying.
💡 Pool + teen driver = $2M umbrella minimum recommendation every time
6
E&O / Professional Tab
Analyze professional liability for any profession. The Agent E&O Self-Audit is specifically built for licensed AZ producers — run it quarterly to verify documentation practices, retroactive dates, and tail coverage. D&O analysis covers directors of non-profits and corporate boards.
💡 Claims-made retroactive date is the most commonly overlooked E&O gap — verify it covers the full policy period
7
AZ Risk Tab
Enter ZIP code and property details for an AZ-specific risk profile. The Catastrophic Risk Map summarizes the 5 most common AZ P&C exposures. Use this tab to educate clients on why AZ has unique insurance needs that mainland carriers don't always understand.
8
Claims ROI Tab
Enter annual premium, 3-year claims, and deductible. The live calculator shows loss ratio, average annual claim cost, and net annual cost. The AI renewal strategy tool uses this data to recommend whether to increase deductibles, self-fund small claims, or negotiate renewal terms. The deductible optimization table is a client education tool.
💡 A loss ratio over 60% triggers carrier scrutiny — recommend claim frequency reduction strategies proactively
