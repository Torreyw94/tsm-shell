FLSA — Fair Labor Standards Act, 29 U.S.C. § 207
Federal · Department of Labor
Requires covered employers to pay eligible employees at least 1.5x their regular rate for hours worked over 40 in a workweek. Exemptions apply for executive, administrative, professional, and outside sales employees meeting salary and duties tests.
ARS § 23-1501 — Arizona Employment Relationship
Arizona · Employment Law
Establishes at-will employment doctrine in Arizona. Exceptions include written employment contracts, implied contracts, and statutory protections against wrongful termination for protected class members or whistleblowers.
