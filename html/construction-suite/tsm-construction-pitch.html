02   The Problem

Every GC Is Running Blind
Between the Field and the Office.

By the time a cost overrun appears in your report, it's already weeks old. By the time a lien waiver gap surfaces, the sub has already been paid. The tools exist — the intelligence layer doesn't.

💸 Cost Overruns Caught Too Late
Cost reports land at month-end. By then the concrete sub has poured 3 extra yards, the steel delay has compounded, and the CO is already in dispute. The damage is done.
Avg exposure: $142K/project
📄 Lien Waiver Gaps
Subs get paid. Waivers don't always follow. One missing unconditional waiver from a tier-2 sub can cloud title and delay project close for months. Nobody tracks this in real time.
$128K average lien exposure
📅 Schedule Slippage → LD Risk
An 11-day slip at $2,500/day is $27,500 in exposure — before you know which days are excusable. Without daily critical-path scoring, you're guessing on every extension request.
$27,500 current LD exposure
🛡 Compliance Blindspots
Permits expire. Inspections get missed. Certified payroll falls behind. One NOV can trigger a stop-work order that costs more than a month of superintendent salary.
1 stop-work = $40K+/day loss
💰 Slow Draws = Slow Cash
Draw requests assembled manually take days. Every week a G702 sits unsigned is a week of float you're funding out of your line of credit. $287K sitting unsubmitted right now.
$287K in submittable draws
📋 RFIs Age Into Claims
RFI-142 is 24 days old. No response from the EOR. Zero float on the critical path. Every day that RFI ages is a day of excusable delay you're not documenting for your extension request.
24-day RFI · $0 documented
