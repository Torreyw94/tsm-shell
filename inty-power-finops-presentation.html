THE CHALLENGE
Where solar accounting
breaks down without the right tools.
01
Per Install
JOB COST COMPLEXITY
Each install has 8–12 cost components: panels, inverters, batteries, racking, labor, permits, HOA fees, utility fees, financing costs. Without AI tracking, margin bleeds silently.
02
30% ITC
TAX CREDIT DOCUMENTATION
The federal Investment Tax Credit is 30% of install cost — but only if documented correctly per IRS Rev. Proc. 2007-65. Missed documentation = missed credits = real dollar losses.
03
Multi-Source
REVENUE RECOGNITION
Cash sales, financing plans, APS/SRP rebates, SREC income — all recognized differently. Without proper month-end processes, financial statements are unreliable for growth decisions.
The bottom line: Inty Power's direct-to-consumer model with in-house installation creates high-volume, project-based accounting complexity. A Staff Accountant with AI-powered FinOps tools doesn't just keep up — they get ahead of the margin leakage before it happens.
