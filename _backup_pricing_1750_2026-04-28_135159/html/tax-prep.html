AI-Powered Tax Intelligence for Individuals, Businesses & Corporations
◆ PLATFORM OVERVIEW
AuditOps Pro is a multi-domain AI tax intelligence platform powered by the TSM Neural Processor running on the TSM AI backend. It covers all entity types — individuals (1040), S-Corps (1120-S), C-Corps (1120), partnerships (1065), trusts (1041), and estate/gift tax (706/709). The AI is trained on 2025 tax rules and IRS regulations, and will automatically detect tax cliffs (income thresholds where your tax liability spikes) and tax benefits (deductions and credits you may be missing).
⚡
Multi-Domain Analysis
The flagship mode. Toggle domain connectors on the left sidebar to select which areas of your financial life to analyze. The AI cross-references all active domains simultaneously to find intersections and optimization opportunities a single-domain review would miss.
📋
Tax Return Prep
Enter your actual income and deduction figures for a line-by-line 2025 return analysis. Supports all filing types and schedules. The AI compares standard vs. itemized deductions, estimates your refund or balance due, and flags every cliff and benefit at your exact income level.
🔍
Audit Presets
9 professional audit frameworks for high-risk areas: S-Corp compensation, QBI deductions, cost segregation, R&D credits, FIRPTA, partnership returns, estate tax, NIIT, and entity comparison. Paste raw client data or financials into the context box for a document-aware analysis.
AI POWERS EVERY OUTPUT
Every analysis, return prep, and audit preset result is generated live by the AI. The TSM Neural Processor model runs via the TSM backend. It applies 2025 tax law, IRS regulations, and your specific inputs in real-time — no pre-written templates.
RELAY TO STRATEGIST
After any analysis, click Relay to Strategist to send the full output to strategist.tsmatter.com with the context pre-filled. The Strategist AI will then provide cross-domain recommendations beyond the scope of tax alone — business structure, timing, cash flow, and more.
⚠ TAX CLIFFS
Red alerts that appear automatically when your income is near a threshold that triggers a higher tax rate, phaseout, or surtax. Key 2025 cliffs: $200K NIIT · $197,300 QBI phaseout · $609,350 top bracket · $518,900 cap gains 20%. Cliffs appear in real-time as you type in the Tax Return Prep form.
✓ TAX BENEFITS
Green alerts that fire when your income qualifies for a special benefit. Examples: 0% cap gains rate below $44,025 · Full QBI deduction below $197,300 · SS wage base strategy below $168,600. These appear live in the Return Prep tab and are flagged in every AI analysis output.
