03 — Presentation plan

The 5-step pitch that works for every sector

STEP 01

The problem slide

Open with their specific pain point and a dollar figure. "Your analysts spend X hours/month reviewing documents that cost $Y/hr. Here's what gets missed." Make them nod before you show anything.

STEP 02

The live demo

No slides. Open the relevant app. Load their document type. Run analysis live while they watch. Let the AI produce the report in real time. The silence while it runs is the best sales moment you'll ever have.

STEP 03

The proof point

Show Banner Health, HonorHealth, or Desert Financial as live deployments. "This is already running for [their peer]. They found $X in compliance gaps in the first month." Real clients close deals.

STEP 04

The ROI math

"You pay $X/mo. You replace Y analyst hours at $Z/hr. Payback in [weeks]. After that it's pure savings." Make them do the math out loud. When they calculate it themselves, they sell themselves.

STEP 05

The frictionless close

"30-day pilot at $2,500. No contract. If it doesn't save you more than it costs in the first month, cancel." Remove every objection before it's raised. First deal is the proof of concept.

UPSELL

White-label (after close)

"We can brand this as [Client Name] AI. Your clients see your brand. You charge them a markup. We handle all infrastructure and updates." Only mention after they've said yes to the pilot.

Rule: Every pitch is the same 5 steps. The only thing that changes is which app you open and which document type you load. You never need a new deck — just the right app and a real document.
