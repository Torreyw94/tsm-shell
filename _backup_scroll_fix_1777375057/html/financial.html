HOW-TO · DOCUMENTATION
Platform guide · AI capabilities · use cases
How do I use the Preset Library? ▼
Click any preset in the left sidebar to load a pre-configured AI prompt into the active tab's input. The AI will auto-run the analysis. You can modify the prompt before sending or type directly in the input bar for custom queries.
What AI model powers this platform? ▼
TSM Financial Intelligence Pro is powered by Groq's llama-3.3-70b-versatile model via the TSM Financial Processor spoke. The system uses a specialized financial system prompt calibrated for CFO-level analysis, audit work, and regulatory compliance.
How do I run an Audit Engine scan? ▼
Navigate to the Audit Engine tab. You can click "RUN AUDIT SCAN" for a general compliance review, or type a specific audit scope (e.g., "SOX 404 walkthrough for revenue cycle") in the input field. Use the sidebar presets like "SOX Controls Audit" for structured analysis.
Can I analyze specific invoices or reports? ▼
Yes. In the Invoices tab, click "AI ANALYZE" for an automatic aging/collections analysis, or type specific questions about your receivables. In the Reports tab, click "VIEW AI SUMMARY" next to any report for an instant AI-generated executive summary.
What frameworks does the platform cover? ▼
Active frameworks include SOX 302/404 (SOX 2.0), IFRS 17 (Insurance Contracts), US GAAP (Revenue Recognition, ASC 606), PCAOB AS 2201 (Internal Controls), Basel III (Capital Adequacy), and ESG/AI Governance overlays. The AI is calibrated for all of these regulatory environments.
How is the Health Score calculated? ▼
The Health Score (0–100) is a composite index covering: Liquidity (20%), Leverage (20%), Profitability (20%), Compliance posture (20%), and Audit readiness (20%). Each dimension is scored against industry benchmarks and weighted by materiality. Click "RUN DIAGNOSTIC" for a full AI breakdown.
