How To Use This App
Complete guide for every feature · Arizona-licensed agents · NPN 18818059
⚙ First: Configure Groq AI
1
Get Your Free Groq API Key

Click the Configure Groq AI button in the top-right corner. Paste your key (starts with gsk_) and click Save & Activate. The button turns green when active.
💡 Your key is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it is never transmitted anywhere except directly to Groq's API.
♡ Life Insurance Tab
1
DIME Needs Calculator
Enter the client's annual income, years to replace, total debt, mortgage balance, education fund goal, and any existing coverage. The calculator instantly computes recommended coverage using the industry-standard DIME method.
💡 Most clients underestimate by 5–10× because they only think about burial costs. DIME reveals the true gap.
2
AI Analyze This
After running the DIME calculator, click ◌ AI Analyze This. Groq will generate a personalized recommendation including policy type selection, carrier strategy for Arizona, and rider suggestions.
3
Policy Estimator
Enter age, gender, coverage amount, health class, tobacco status, and term length. Three plan cards update in real-time: Term Life, Whole Life, and Final Expense. Click AI Compare Plans for a detailed comparison narrative.
💡 Rates are illustrative. Always quote formally through carrier raters. AZ DOI requires written disclosure of all policy terms.
4
Save to Report
Click ＋ Save to Report to add the DIME analysis or plan estimate to your Reports tab. Reports can be printed or downloaded as a PDF for client delivery.
✚ Health / ACA Tab
1
APTC Estimator
Enter household size, annual MAGI, ages in household, and county/ZIP. The calculator computes FPL%, maximum monthly APTC, benchmark premium (2nd-lowest Silver), and after-APTC cost using 2025 ACA parameters.
⚠ Always warn clients about APTC clawback. If MAGI exceeds estimate at year-end, IRS recaptures the excess. Especially risky for gig workers, self-employed, and those with variable income.
2
CSR Alert Logic
When a client is between 100–250% FPL, the calculator automatically alerts you to recommend a Silver plan. CSR on Silver can reduce deductibles from $7,500 to as low as $150 — at no additional premium cost.
3
SEP Qualifier Cards
Eight qualifying life event cards are displayed. Click any card to highlight it (border turns gold). Use these to quickly assess client eligibility. Click AI: Determine My Client's SEP Eligibility to get a detailed Groq analysis with documentation requirements.
★ Medicare Tab
1
Parts A/B/C/D Reference Cards
Click any Part card (A, B, C, D) to expand a detailed reference panel with 2025 costs, rules, and AZ-specific notes. This is your quick reference during client meetings — no need to search CMS.gov.
2
IRMAA Calculator
Enter 2023 MAGI (used for 2025 IRMAA determination) and filing status. The calculator shows the surcharge, total Part B premium, and annual financial impact. Click AI IRMAA Planning for Roth conversion and income-management strategies.
💡 IRMAA is determined two years in advance. A client converting a large IRA in 2023 will face IRMAA in 2025 — warn proactively.
3
MA vs. Medigap Decision Tool
Side-by-side comparison of Medicare Advantage vs. Medigap Plan G with AZ carrier callouts and use-case recommendations. Click AI: MA vs Medigap for My Client to get a personalized recommendation based on your client's profile.
4
LIS / Extra Help Screener
Enter income, assets, and household size. The screener computes eligibility for Low Income Subsidy (Extra Help) — reducing Part D costs to near-zero for qualifying beneficiaries. Never leave a dual-eligible without checking LIS.
